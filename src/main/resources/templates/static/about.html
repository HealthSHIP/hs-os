About HealthSHIP

Health Students Helping In Pandemics

Hi! We are HealthSHIP, a student-led social movement rooted in compassion for the health of our communities.

We aim to support health and social care staff with everyday tasks from caring responsibilities to grocery shopping. We believe the impact of COVID-19 will span the entirety of our social fabrics and that each have a role to play in the hindrance of this pandemic.

Students may not be able to work on the front line yet, but we can support those that do.
